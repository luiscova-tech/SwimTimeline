# Time Standards Sources

Benchmarks in `../extracted/mila-timeline.md` and `../extracted/mila-events.json` use:

- USA Swimming 2024-2028 Single Age Motivational Standards, 12 Girls LCM: https://www.usaswimming.org/docs/default-source/timesdocuments/time-standards/2025/2028-motivational-standards-single-age.pdf
- AZSI 2025-2026 Age Group State Qualifying Time Standards, Women 11-12 LCM: https://www.azswimming.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/AZSI-Age-Group-State-Qualifying-Time-Standards-2025-2026.pdf
- AZSI 2025-2026 Age Group Regional Qualifying Time Standards, Women 11-12 LCM: https://www.azswimming.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/AZSI-Age-Group-Regional-Qualifying-Time-Standards-2025-2026.pdf

Standards were matched by swimmer age, gender, course, event, and verified seed time.
